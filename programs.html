Did You Know?

Teaching music to young children and infants has been proven to help them improve learning, memory, reasoning abilities and general creativity, in addition to disciplinary skills needed later in life.

Programs

Music For Little Mozarts Group Class - Ages 4-6
$160.00 for 10 week session (Group Classroom Course Only)

Follow the adventures of Mozart Mouse and Beethoven Bear as they go on a musical journey, introducing music to young children through movement, song and fun activities that help children enjoy developing physical, emotional, social and academic skills.

Along the way, Mozart Mouse and Beethoven Bear will also meet some other musical friends such as Clara Schumann-Cat and Pachelbel Penguin. Shhhhh!! Don't tell anyone, but sometimes they even make an appearance at the class!!

The Music For Little Mozarts program is taught in a group setting, students do not need an instrument, and parents are strongly encouraged to particpate with their child. Class location and start dates to be determined based on enrolment.

Length of Course: The group course runs for 10 weeks and each class is held on a weekly basis. After completion of the first 10 week course, parents have the option of continuing their child on with the second and third parts of the course, each running for 10 weeks.

1-on-1 Instrument Instruction - Ages 6-99
$25.00 per half hour
$40.00 per hour

Alexander offers 1-on-1 private lessons for the following instruments: Piano, Clarinet, Saxophone and the Recorder.

Using popular curriculum from the Faber Piano Adventures, various methods published by Alfred's Music, including the non-group version of Music For Little Mozarts and other curriculum, the student receives a well-rounded music education.

Please note, the suggested methods can vary between students, their age, what level they are at and what their personal goals may be. Alexander uses certain methods as they can provide a great foundation for moving towards other popular methods such as The Royal Conservatory of Music (RCM) curriculum.

Private Instruction takes place once per week and are offered in your own home!

Music Theory
$25.00 per half hour
$40.00 per hour

Theory is offered along with most of the popular published methods and is also used in conjunction with some lessons, however theory specific lessons are available if the student wishes to learn more or needs some tutoring.

Theory lessons take place once per week, or as needed, and are offered in your own home!
